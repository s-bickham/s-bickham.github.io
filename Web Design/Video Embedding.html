How to Embed a Video into a Webpage

This tutorial will help you through the steps of embedding a video into your webpage.

Step 1: Find the Video

Go to the video you want to embed on YouTube

Step 2: Click the Share Button

Under the video click on the Share button.

Step 3: Select the Embed Option

Choose the Embed option from the list that appears.

Step 4: Copy the Embed Code

Copy the HTML code shown.

Step 5: Put the Embed Code into Your Webpage

Put the embed code into your HTML file where you want the video to show up.
